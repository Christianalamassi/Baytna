BAYTNA - BAYTKUM

BAYTNA - BAYTKUM

Welcome! Make yourselves at home! Baytna baytkum is Arabic and means "Our home is your home."
Everyone should feel comfortable at Baytna.
Baytna is a project by the organization HOPE e.V. and was founded in 2025 in Berlin-Kreuzberg.
We offer groups, courses, and events for men, women, children, and families.
Our goal is for people of all backgrounds who live in Berlin to truly feel at home in the city.
Coming from different cultures, we want to learn new things together, make friends, and build a sense of community.
